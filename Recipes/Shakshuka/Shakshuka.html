Shakshuka

Image from Yoav Aziz on Unsplash

Description

Shakshuka is a popular Middle Eastern and north African breakfast dish comprising of poached eggs in a bold tomato sauce that's seasoned with cumin, turmeric, and jalapeno peppers. The recipe can be varied by flavouring the tomato sauce with curry powder, pesto, spices or fresh herbs of choice. It's rich, flavourful, and a breeze to make!

Time: 30 mins

2 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup chopped onions
  • 3 cloves chopped garlic
  • 1/2 green jalapeno peppers
  • 14 oz can diced tomato
  • 4 raw eggs
  • 3 tbs olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt or to taste
  • 1 tsp cumin or to taste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • Chili powder or black pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsely or cilantro

Optional

  • Grated chedder cheese
  • Fried mushrooms
  • Sliced avocado
  • Chicken sausages

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large saut√© pan on medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and cook until translucent.
  3. Add garlic and spices and cook for approximately one minute
  4. Pour the can of tomatoes and break down the tomatoes using a large spoon.
  5. Add jalapeno peppers and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Bring the sauce to a simmer until preferred consistency.
  7. Use large spoon to make small wells in the tomato sauce and crack the eggs into each well. Cover the pan and cook for 5 minutes or until the eggs are poached to desired consistency.
  8. Garnish with coriander or parsley.
  9. Carefully remove the eggs from the pan and serve with the spicy tomato sauce and bread.
